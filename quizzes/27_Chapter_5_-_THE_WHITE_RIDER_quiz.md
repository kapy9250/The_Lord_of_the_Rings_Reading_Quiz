# Reading Comprehension Quiz: 27_Chapter_5_-_THE_WHITE_RIDER.md

**Difficulty:** Advanced | **Questions:** 10 | **Time suggested:** 30–40 minutes

**Instructions:** Each question below is followed by a relevant passage from the text. Choose the single best answer (A–E) for each question. Focus on what the text *implies* rather than what it explicitly states. Consider tone, subtext, character psychology, and authorial choices in your reasoning.

---

### Question 1 `[CIM/SCA]` `[Advanced]`

- **A.** Aragorn’s lack of practical experience with animals.
- **B.** His developing intuition that the "old man" seen in the night may not have been the malevolent force they initially feared, but rather a restorative presence recognized by the natural world.
- **C.** A sign that the horses are actually spies for Saruman.
- **D.** Legolas’s desire to comfort Aragorn with a happy lie.
- **E.** That the horses have finally escaped their duty to the Men of Rohan.
