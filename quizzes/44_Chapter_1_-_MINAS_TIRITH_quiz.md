# Reading Comprehension Quiz: 44_Chapter_1_-_MINAS_TIRITH.md

**Difficulty:** Advanced | **Questions:** 10 | **Time suggested:** 30–40 minutes

**Instructions:** Each question below is followed by a relevant passage from the text. Choose the single best answer (A–E) for each question. Focus on what the text *implies* rather than what it explicitly states. Consider tone, subtext, character psychology, and authorial choices in your reasoning.

---

### Question 1 `[CIM/ANT]` `[Advanced]`

- **A.** A sign of his boredom with the journey.
- **B.** A state of sensory and temporal dislocation caused by the supernatural speed of Shadowfax and the psychological weight of the quest, where the normal boundaries of time and space have been dissolved into a continuous, "dream-like" flight.
- **C.** Evidence that Pippin is suffering from a literal physical illness.
- **D.** His belief that Gandalf is actually a very slow horseman.
- **E.** A sign that Pippin is losing his mind due to the Palantír.
