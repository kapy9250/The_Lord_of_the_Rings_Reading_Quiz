# Reading Comprehension Quiz: 29_Chapter_7_-_HELMS_DEEP.md

**Difficulty:** Advanced | **Questions:** 10 | **Time suggested:** 30–40 minutes

**Instructions:** Each question below is followed by a relevant passage from the text. Choose the single best answer (A–E) for each question. Focus on what the text *implies* rather than what it explicitly states. Consider tone, subtext, character psychology, and authorial choices in your reasoning.

---

### Question 1 `[ACN/ANT]` `[Advanced]`

The "veiling shadow" that Legolas sees approaching from Isengard, described as "twilight under endless trees... flowing downwards from the hills," primarily serves to:

> **Relevant Passage:**
> "'I can see a darkness. ... It is not mist or cloud that defeats my eyes: there is a veiling shadow that some power lays upon the land... It is as if the twilight under endless trees were flowing downwards from the hills.'"

- **A.** Predict a heavy rainstorm that will hinder the Riders.
- **B.** Manifest the psychological and physical "reach" of Saruman's sorcery, characterizing his power as a corrosive, unnatural twilight that consumes the clarity of the natural world.
- **C.** Show that Legolas needs a rest from the long ride.
- **D.** Signal the arrival of the Elves of Mirkwood.
- **E.** Explain why the Orcs are able to travel in the afternoon.
