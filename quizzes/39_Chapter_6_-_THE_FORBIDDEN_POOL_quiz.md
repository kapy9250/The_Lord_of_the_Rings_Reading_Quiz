# Reading Comprehension Quiz: 39_Chapter_6_-_THE_FORBIDDEN_POOL.md

**Difficulty:** Advanced | **Questions:** 10 | **Time suggested:** 30–40 minutes

**Instructions:** Each question below is followed by a relevant passage from the text. Choose the single best answer (A–E) for each question. Focus on what the text *implies* rather than what it explicitly states. Consider tone, subtext, character psychology, and authorial choices in your reasoning.

---

### Question 1 `[CIM/ANT]` `[Advanced]`

> **Relevant Passage:**

- **A.** Show off his knowledge of the local landscape.
- **B.** Create a shared moment of high-aesthetic and spiritual beauty that grounds their relationship in a mutual appreciation for the "White" world, providing a peaceful prelude to a difficult moral choice.
- **C.** Distract Frodo while the Rangers capture Gollum.
- **D.** Inform Frodo that it is time to leave Henneth Annûn.

### Question 3 `[CIM/PIS]` `[Advanced]`
