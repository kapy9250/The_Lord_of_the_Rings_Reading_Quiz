### Question 1 `[CIM/SCA]` `[Advanced]`

**Celeborn’s offer to let the Company stay in Lorien serves to:**

**Correct Answer: B. Test the moral resolve of each member, ensuring that the Company continues not by obligation, but as a voluntary act of will at the "edge of doom."**

**Why this is correct:**
Celeborn presents a stark choice. This offer acts as a final filter. By allowing a "refuge," he ensures that their participation is a voluntary act of will. This confirms the internal strength of the group before they enter the "doom" ahead.

**Analysis of Distractors:**
- **A** is a "Surface-Level Literalism" about "resources" and "population."
- **C** is a "Plausible Misreading" that suspects a "tactical escape-clause" for Boromir.
- **D** is an "Over-Interpretation" calling the Quest a "spiritual punishment."
- **E** is a "Moralistic Misreading" about a "lack of urgency."

### Question 3 `[PIS/SCA]` `[Advanced]`

**The description of *lembas* characterizes Elven "craft" as:**

**Correct Answer: B. A synthesis of physical and spiritual sustenance, where material objects are infused with a moral and biological potency intended for high-stakes survival.**

**Why this is correct:**
The Elves explain *lembas* is for "when all else fails." It is more than food; it's a "blessing" in material form. The instructions to "eat little" mark it as a sacred or high-order substance, distinguishing Elven "making" from purely utilitarian food.

**Analysis of Distractors:**
- **A** is a "Surface-Level Literalism" about "culinary technology" and "flavor."
- **C** is an "Over-Interpretation" calling it a "deceptive narcotic."
- **D** is an "Academic Over-Interpretation" using the term "secularizing a sacrament."
- **E** is a "Plausible Misreading" about "running out of ingredients."

### Question 4 `[ACN/ANT]` `[Advanced]`

**The shifting colors of the Elven-cloaks symbolize:**

**Correct Answer: B. The profound harmony between Elven skill and the natural world, allowing the wearer to become a literal extension of the environment’s own shifting textures.**

**Why this is correct:**
The cloaks have the "hue and beauty of all these things" (leaf, branch, water, stone). This reflects Elvish philosophy: putting "thought of all that we love into all that we make." The cloaks provide protection through "harmony" rather than "armor."

**Analysis of Distractors:**
- **A** is a "Plausible Misreading" about "instability of preferences."
- **C** is a "Surface-Level Literalism" about "color-blindness."
- **D** is an "Over-Interpretation" calling it a "glamour to hide thinning."
- **E** is a "Moralistic Misreading" about "hiding unnatural beauty."

### Question 9 `[ACN/ANT]` `[Advanced]`
