# Reading Comprehension Quiz: 54_Chapter_1_-_THE_TOWER_OF_CIRITH_UNGOL.md

**Difficulty:** Advanced | **Questions:** 10 | **Time suggested:** 30–40 minutes

**Instructions:** Each question below is followed by a relevant passage from the text. Choose the single best answer (A–E) for each question. Focus on what the text *implies* rather than what it explicitly states. Consider tone, subtext, character psychology, and authorial choices in your reasoning.

---

### Question 1 `[CIM/ANT]` `[Advanced]`

Sam’s realization that "time was desperately precious" but that "perishing is more likely" reflects a character state of:

> **Relevant Passage:**
> "He no longer had any doubt about his duty: he must rescue his master or perish in the attempt. 'The perishing is more likely, and will be a lot easier anyway,' he said grimly to himself..."

- **A.** A suicidal desire to end his suffering.
- **B.** A "High" heroic realism that accepts the near-certainty of death while remaining absolutely committed to a moral duty, choosing the "hard" path of action over the "easy" path of surrender.
- **C.** Evidence that Sam is becoming as cynical as Denethor.
- **D.** His belief that the Orcs will kill him very quickly.
- **E.** A sign that Sam has lost his sense of duty to the Shire.

### Question 2 `[ACN/ANT]` `[Advanced]`

### Question 3 `[CIM/PIS]` `[Advanced]`

### Question 4 `[CIM/SCA]` `[Advanced]`

### Question 6 `[ACN/ANT]` `[Advanced]`
