# Reading Comprehension Quiz: 57_Chapter_4_-_THE_FIELD_OF_CORMALLEN.md

**Difficulty:** Advanced | **Questions:** 10 | **Time suggested:** 30–40 minutes

**Instructions:** Each question below is followed by a relevant passage from the text. Choose the single best answer (A–E) for each question. Focus on what the text *implies* rather than what it explicitly states. Consider tone, subtext, character psychology, and authorial choices in your reasoning.

---

### Question 1 `[ACN/ANT]` `[Advanced]`

> **Relevant Passage:**

- **A.** The literal death of Sauron in a final combat.
- **B.** The total, ontological dissolution of Sauron's malicious "presence" and the end of his spiritual dominance over the physical world, characterized as a final, impotent gesture of defiance before his utter fading.
- **C.** A sign that the weather in Mordor is finally improving.
- **D.** Evidence that the Orcs are using smoke-machines to hide their retreat.
- **E.** A humorous comparison to the clouds of the Shire.

### Question 6 `[CIM/ANT]` `[Advanced]`
