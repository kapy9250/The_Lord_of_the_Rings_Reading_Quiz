# Reading Comprehension Quiz: 04_Chapter_4_-_A_SHORT_CUT_TO_MUSHROOMS.md

**Difficulty:** Advanced | **Questions:** 10 | **Time suggested:** 30–40 minutes

**Instructions:** Each question below is followed by a relevant passage from the text. Choose the single best answer (A–E) for each question. Focus on what the text *implies* rather than what it explicitly states. Consider tone, subtext, character psychology, and authorial choices in your reasoning.

---

### Question 1 `[CIM/ANT]` `[Advanced]`

> "From Frodo's mind the bright morning - treacherously bright, he thought - had not banished the fear of pursuit; and he pondered the words of Gildor."

- **A.** He is suffering from a headache due to sleeping on a tree-root.
- **B.** He perceives a disconnect between the beautiful, peaceful environment and the hidden, deadly danger he faces.
- **C.** He believes that the sun is actually a spy for the Black Riders.
- **D.** He is annoyed that Pippin is in such high spirits while he is worried.
- **E.** He regrets leaving the bower and wishes he could stay in the wood forever.
