# Reading Comprehension Quiz: 36_Chapter_3_-_THE_BLACK_GATE_IS_CLOSED.md

**Difficulty:** Advanced | **Questions:** 10 | **Time suggested:** 30–40 minutes

**Instructions:** Each question below is followed by a relevant passage from the text. Choose the single best answer (A–E) for each question. Focus on what the text *implies* rather than what it explicitly states. Consider tone, subtext, character psychology, and authorial choices in your reasoning.

---

### Question 1 `[SCA/ACN]` `[Advanced]`

- **A.** The high architectural skill of the Men of Gondor.

- **C.** A sign that Gondor is planning to take the towers back soon.
- **D.** Evidence that the Orcs are better at repairing buildings than Men are.
- **E.** A humorous example of how quickly property ownership can change in war.
