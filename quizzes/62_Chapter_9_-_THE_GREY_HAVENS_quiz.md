# Reading Comprehension Quiz: 62_Chapter_9_-_THE_GREY_HAVENS.md

**Difficulty:** Advanced | **Questions:** 10 | **Time suggested:** 30–40 minutes

**Instructions:** Each question below is followed by a relevant passage from the text. Choose the single best answer (A–E) for each question. Focus on what the text *implies* rather than what it explicitly states. Consider tone, subtext, character psychology, and authorial choices in your reasoning.

### Question 1 `[CIM/ANT]` `[Advanced]`

Lobelia Bracegirdle’s decision to leave her money to help "hobbits made homeless by the troubles" represents:

> **Relevant Passage:**
> "she had left all that remained of her money... for him to use in helping hobbits made homeless... So that feud was ended."

- **A.** A strategic attempt to repair her reputation and secure a "High" place in Shire-history after years of greed.
- **B.** A profound moral transformation and sacrificial reconciliation, where her suffering in the Lockholes has refined her into a figure of late-life generosity, ending her lifelong obsession with possession.
- **C.** Evidence that she was actually a secret supporter of Frodo’s quest from the beginning.
- **D.** A plan to spite her Bracegirdle relatives by denying them their inheritance.
- **E.** A sign that the "Shadow" has made her forget the importance of property.
