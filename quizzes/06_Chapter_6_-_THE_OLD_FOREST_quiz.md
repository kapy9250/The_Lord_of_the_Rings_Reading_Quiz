# Reading Comprehension Quiz: 06_Chapter_6_-_THE_OLD_FOREST.md

**Difficulty:** Advanced | **Questions:** 10 | **Time suggested:** 30–40 minutes

**Instructions:** Each question below is followed by a relevant passage from the text. Choose the single best answer (A–E) for each question. Focus on what the text *implies* rather than what it explicitly states. Consider tone, subtext, character psychology, and authorial choices in your reasoning.

---

### Question 1 `[SCA/ANT]` `[Advanced]`

- **A.** A misunderstanding caused by the hobbits' lack of botanical knowledge.
- **B.** A deep-seated, reciprocal enmity based on mutual territorial infringement and violent retaliation.
- **C.** A legend invented by the Bucklanders to keep children away from the Hedge.
- **D.** Evidence that the trees are under the direct control of the Dark Lord.
- **E.** A temporary environmental imbalance caused by a particularly dry summer.
